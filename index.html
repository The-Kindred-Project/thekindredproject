Our Purpose

Education is a right,
not a privilege.

The Kindred Project was born from a simple belief: every young person, regardless of where they were born, deserves access to quality education and the opportunity to shape their own future. We work at the intersection of policy, advocacy, and youth empowerment to turn that belief into action.
